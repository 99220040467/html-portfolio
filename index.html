Food Safety

"Food safety refers to the procedures and practices put in place to ensure that food is safe to eat. This involves everything from the handling and preparation
of food to its storage and consumption. The goal is to prevent foodborne illnes
-ses and ensure that food does not pose a risk to human health. Key aspects
of food safety include maintaining cleanliness, preventing cross-contaminatio
-n, cooking food thoroughly, keeping perishable items properly refrigerated, following hygiene practices, and adhering to food storage guidelines. By imple
-menting these measures, we can safeguard public health and enjoy food without worrying about potential risks."
